The City Plan - Rajpur

My team is working on an ambitious and sustainable city planning project for Rajpur, aimed at enhancing the quality of life for its residents. The plan includes the development of efficient public transportation systems, abundant green spaces, and modern residential areas. Environmental experts are focusing on waste management solutions, while urban planners are ensuring that the city's infrastructure integrates seamlessly with local businesses and community needs. Through collaboration and community feedback, the team is shaping Rajpur into a vibrant, eco-friendly, and well-connected city.
